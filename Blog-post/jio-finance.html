What is Jio Finance & How to Open a Savings Account in Jio Payment Bank

What is Jio Finance?

Jio Finance is a digital financial services platform launched by Reliance Jio, a part of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL). It aims to offer a wide range of digital financial services including payments, banking, lending, insurance, and investment solutions—all in one place.

Jio Finance leverages Jio's strong digital ecosystem and customer base to provide seamless, secure, and convenient financial services, especially focusing on financial inclusion for underserved and rural populations of India.

As of now, Jio Finance includes services like:

  • Jio Payment Bank
  • UPI payments and QR codes
  • Recharge and bill payments
  • Digital lending (coming soon)
  • Insurance and investments (future plans)

What is Jio Payment Bank?

Jio Payment Bank is a digital bank set up by Reliance Industries in partnership with the State Bank of India (SBI). It operates as a payments bank under the guidelines of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). A payments bank offers basic banking services like:

  • Savings and current accounts
  • UPI and mobile payments
  • Debit card services
  • Online banking and mobile banking

Jio Payment Bank does not offer credit services like loans or credit cards, but it's perfect for secure digital transactions, small savings, and day-to-day financial activities.

Benefits of Opening a Savings Account in Jio Payment Bank

  • Zero balance account
  • No minimum deposit required
  • Free virtual debit card
  • Quick and paperless account opening
  • Secure and instant UPI transactions
  • Easy access via the MyJio app

How to Open a Savings Account in Jio Payment Bank

Follow these simple steps to open a Jio Payment Bank savings account:

  1. Download the MyJio App:
    Visit the Google Play Store or Apple App Store and install the MyJio app on your smartphone.
  2. Login or Register:
    Open the app and log in using your Jio mobile number. If you are a new user, register using your mobile number and OTP verification.
  3. Navigate to Jio Payment Bank:
    On the home screen, look for the option "Jio Payment Bank" or "Finance" section.
  4. Choose ‘Open Savings Account’:
    Click on the “Open Account” button and proceed.
  5. Enter Required Details:
    Fill in your personal details like name, Aadhaar number, PAN card, and address.
  6. Complete KYC:
    Complete your e-KYC through Aadhaar OTP or by scheduling a visit with a representative for full KYC.
  7. Set MPIN:
    Set a secure MPIN for accessing your account and making transactions.
  8. Account Activated:
    Once verification is complete, your Jio Payment Bank savings account will be active and ready to use.

Conclusion

Jio Finance and Jio Payment Bank are transforming the way digital financial services are delivered in India. With the backing of Reliance and SBI, users can expect reliable, fast, and secure services. Opening a savings account in Jio Payment Bank is extremely simple and convenient through the MyJio app.

Whether you're a student, working professional, or a small business owner, Jio Payment Bank can serve your everyday banking needs without any hassle.
