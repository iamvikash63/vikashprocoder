What is Jio Finance & How to Open a Savings Account in Jio Payment Bank

What is Jio Finance?

Jio Finance is a digital financial services platform developed by Reliance Jio, a subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited. It aspires to provide a comprehensive suite of digital financial services, including payments, banking, lending, insurance, and investing products, all in one place.

Jio Finance leverages Jio's strong digital ecosystem and client base to provide smooth, secure, and convenient financial services, with an emphasis on financial inclusion for India's underserved and rural communities.

Jio Finance currently provides the following services:

  • Jio Payment Bank
  • UPI payments and QR codes
  • Recharge and bill payments
  • Digital lending (coming soon)
  • Insurance and investments (future plans)

What is Jio Payment Bank?

Jio Payment Bank is a digital bank founded by Reliance Industries in collaboration with State Bank of India (SBI). It functions as a payments bank under the supervision of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). A payments bank provides basic banking services, including:

  • Savings and current accounts
  • UPI and mobile payments
  • Debit card services
  • Online banking and mobile banking

Jio Payment Bank does not provide credit services like as loans or credit cards, but it is ideal for secure digital transactions, small deposits, and everyday financial activity.

Benefits of Opening a Savings Account in Jio Payment Bank

  • Zero balance account
  • No minimum deposit required
  • Free virtual debit card
  • Quick and paperless account opening
  • Secure and instant UPI transactions
  • Easy access via the MyJio app

How to Open a Savings Account in Jio Payment Bank

Follow these simple steps to open a Jio Payment Bank savings account:

  1. Download the MyJio app:
    Download the MyJio app from Google Play or Apple App Store for your smartphone.
  2. Login or Register:
    Open the app and log in with your Jio cell number. If you are a first-time user, enter your cellphone number and verify with an OTP.
  3. Navigate to Jio Payment Bank:
    On the home screen, look for the option "Jio Payment Bank" or "Finance" section.
  4. Choose ‘Open Savings Account’:
    Click on the “Open Account” button and proceed.
  5. Enter Required Details:
    Fill in your personal details like name, Aadhaar number, PAN card, and address.
  6. Complete KYC:
    Complete your e-KYC using Aadhaar OTP or by making an appointment with a representative for full KYC.
  7. Set MPIN:
    Set a secure MPIN for accessing your account and making transactions.
  8. Account Activated:
    Once verified, your Jio Payment Bank savings account will be activated and ready for usage.

Conclusion

Jio Finance and Jio Payment Bank are changing how digital financial services are delivered in India. Users may expect reliable, fast, and secure services from Reliance and SBI. Opening a savings account with Jio Payment Bank is incredibly straightforward and convenient using the MyJio app.

Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a small company owner, Jio Payment Bank can meet your everyday banking needs without trouble.
